Frequently Asked Questions

These are the most common and frequently asked question by the user, which are answerd below.
If the user face any issue there is a help option which can be used from nav bar to mail the admin.

  • What is PCE Cloud?

    Pce Cloud is a free Jupyter notebook environment that runs entirely in the cloud. Most importantly, it does not require a setup and the notebooks that you create can be simultaneously edited by your team members.

  • What is the PCE Cloud uptime ?

    PCE Cloud has approximately 98% uptime. The only point of failure is at network providers end which we are trying to bring to 100%

  • Do I need to visit the college to access the system?

    PCE Cloud is accesible remotly for all users. The User need not come to college for acessing the Cloud he or she can simply acess it by few clicks remotely.

  • Whom to contact incase of any difficulty?

    Incase of any difficulty send a mail to pcecloud@student.mes.ac.in , admin will respond to you as soon as possible.

  • Can people outside MES access the PCE Cloud ?

    People outside the MES can acess the Cloud with proper permission of the Admin .
